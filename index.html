Who am I?

Hello, I am Pauline. I have two years’ experience as a project manager and producer for software and game development projects combined with my Masters degree in Game Design. My goal as a project manager is to always work with empathy and understanding of everyone's needs to deliver a project. I work with cross-functional and fully remote developers creating complex software from inception to launch. My current role as a project analyst requires me to be the primary point of communication between our team and our clients; I define timelines, deliverables, and ensure this is done with our team's wellbeing in mind.

Who am I?

My name is Pauline Ferraro and I am a talented storyteller, researcher and designer. I currently hold two academic degrees. A Bachelors of Arts: English with a minor in Communication from Old Dominion University and a Master’s of Arts degree from the American University School of Communication in Game Design. I have a strong background in creative writing and narrative design work that can be seen below. I have published a novel on Amazon and have made branching narrative games in twine. I have a talent for project management and all the projects listed below I planned and organized deliverables and managed resources and time to ensure project completion. I am open to work in narrative design and project management positions. Please take a look at my resume.

Genesis

Board game created for GAME 601 Game Prototyping. Craft a world though cards and chance. Become the gods of your own world. Win by expertly mapping out a world that gives you the most points based on adjacency and terrain combinations. Combine cards together to create different terrain and place them on your board; be careful natural disasters can hit at any time.

ALONE

A hyperlink fiction game created for GAME 601 Game Prototyping Final Assignment. This game is about depression, agoraphobia and dealing with loss. This game has around four different endings in the form of poems the author has written in her own personal journey with mental illness. This game is a never ending loop with each ending guiding the user back to the start.
